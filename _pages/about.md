# Welcome

I am **Dr. Lijesh Koottaparambil**, a Research Associate at the **Center for Rotating Machinery (CeRoM), Louisiana State University, USA**.

My research focuses on thermodynamic approaches for degradation prediction, structural health monitoring, tribology, hydrogen embrittlement, corrosion, additive manufacturing, molecular dynamics, battery degradation, and intelligent manufacturing systems.

I have authored more than **110 peer-reviewed publications**, contributed to multiple **U.S. and Indian patents**, and serve in associate-editor and editorial-board roles for international journals.

My long-term research vision is to establish **entropy generation as a universal engineering metric** for predicting degradation, failure, and process quality across materials, manufacturing, and energy systems.
