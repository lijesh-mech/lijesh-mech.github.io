# Welcome

I am **Dr. Lijesh Koottaparambil**, Research Associate at the **Center for Rotating Machinery (CeRoM), Louisiana State University (LSU), USA**.

My research focuses on **thermodynamics-based degradation assessment**, **tribology**, **hydrogen embrittlement**, **corrosion**, **molecular dynamics**, **food-process monitoring**, **battery health diagnostics**, and **AI-enabled intelligent manufacturing**.

My work aims to establish **entropy generation** as a universal engineering metric for predicting degradation, remaining useful life, and process quality across diverse engineering systems. I have authored more than **110 peer-reviewed publications**, contributed to multiple **U.S. and Indian patents**, and actively serve the scientific community through editorial and peer-review roles for leading international journals.

This website highlights my research, publications, projects, patents, research-training resources, and professional activities.
