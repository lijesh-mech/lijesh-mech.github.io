Biographical Sketch

Dr. Lijesh Koottaparambil is a Research Associate at the Center for Rotating Machinery (CeRoM), Louisiana State University. His research focuses on thermodynamics-based degradation assessment, tribology, hydrogen embrittlement, corrosion, molecular dynamics, food-process monitoring, battery diagnostics, and intelligent manufacturing.

His work develops physics-informed methods and advanced sensing technologies for evaluating material and system degradation, predicting remaining useful life, and monitoring manufacturing process quality. He has authored more than 110 peer-reviewed publications, contributed to multiple U.S. and international patents, and supports the scientific community through editorial leadership, peer review, and interdisciplinary research collaboration.
